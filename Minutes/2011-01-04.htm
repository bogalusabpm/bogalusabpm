Bogalusa School Board

Bogalusa, Louisiana

Special Board Meeting

January 4, 2011

5:30 P.M.

 

Proceedings Of The Bogalusa School Board Taken At A Special Board Meeting That Was Held on Tuesday, January 4, 2011

 

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a special session at the Bogalusa School Board Office, in the Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana on Tuesday, January 4, 2011, beginning at 5:30P.M., for the sole purpose of Swearing in School Board Members and the Election of Officers for 2011.

 

1.)           Call to Order

Superintendent Ruth Horne presided over the meeting, calling it to order at 5:30 P.M.

 

2.)           Pledge of Allegiance

Superintendent Ruth Horne led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

3.)           Prayer

Reverend Raymond Mims delivered the opening prayer.

 

4.)           Roll Call

There were present: Members:  Mr. Curtis Creel, Mrs. Eleanor Duke, Mr. Calvin Hymel, Mr. Paul Kates, Mr. Adam Kemp, Reverend Raymond Mims and Dr. Brad Williams.

 

There was absent:  None

5.)           Recess to Public Session

There was no one to come forward to address the Board or public.

 

Superintendent Horne resumed regular session.

 

6.)           New Business

 

A.)           Swearing in of 2011 Board Members-Attorney David Merlin Duke-City Prosecutor

Attorney Duke called the School Board Members to the front and gave them their Oath of Office as a group.  Those receiving the Oath of Office were:

 

  • Mr. Curtis Creel

  • Mrs. Eleanor Duke

  • Mr. Calvin Hymel

  • Mr. Paul Kates

  • Mr. Adam Kemp

  • Reverend Raymond Mims

  • Dr. Brad Williams

 

B.)           Election of 2011 Officers

Superintendent Horne opened the nominations for the election of officers as follow:

 

  • Nominations for President

Mr. Adam Kemp nominated Mr. Paul Kates, seconded by Dr. Brad Williams for President.  There being no further nominations for President, the nomination was closed.  The nomination and second for Mr. Paul Kates as President, was unanimous by showing of right hands, with no one opposing.

 

  • Nomination for Vice President

Reverend Raymond Mims nominated Mr. Adam Kemp, seconded by Mr. Paul Kates for Vice President.  There being no further nominations for Vice President, the nomination was closed. The nomination and second for Mr. Adam Kemp as Vice President was unanimous by showing of right hands, with no one opposing.  

 

C.)           Swearing in of 2011 Officers-Attorney David Merlin Duke-City Prosecutor

Attorney Duke gave the Oath of Office to the 2011 Officers as follow:

 

  • Vice President- Mr. Adam Kemp

  • President- Mr. Paul Kates

 

Superintendent Horne congratulated Mr. Kates as President and Mr. Kemp as Vice President to the Board.  She then proceeded with the next order of business.
