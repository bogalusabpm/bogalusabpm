E.  Superintendent Report

 

  • We received funding from Super App from the State Department in the amount of $120,000.00 for strategic planning.

  • Access Health will have a Drug Awareness Day at the high school on May 23, 2024. Students in the 8th - 11th grade may participate. They will work with each grade level for an hour and give them scenarios of entering the emergency room.

  • Discussed a Medical Assistance Program for high school students.

  • Pupil progression informational meeting will be May 9th at 4:30 p.m. It's open to the public. The last day of tutoring is tomorrow (April26, 2024), for the Spring.

  • Summer learning for students is June 3rd - 27th.

  • Summer learning for school leaders is June 3rd-6th.

  • Bogalusa High School's Honors Night is Tuesday, May 14, 2024. Bogalusa High School's graduation is May 17, 2024, at 6:30 p.m.

  • Byrd Avenue Pre-K Promotion Ceremony is May 21, 2024, at 9:00 a.m. The Kindergarten Promotion Ceremony is also May 21, 2024,at 11:00 a.m. Both will be held in the Bogalusa High School's Auditorium.

  • Byrd Avenue will have their 1st Grade Awards Ceremony on May 28, 2024, at 9:00 a.m. and 10:30 a.m. at Byrd Avenue Primary School.

  • Central Elementary School's 5th Grade Promotion is May 22nd at 6:00 p.m. at Bogalusa High School Auditorium.

  • Let's continue to pray for the families of the students we have lost to gun violence.
