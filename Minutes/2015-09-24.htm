Bogalusa School Board Office-Media Center

Bogalusa, Louisiana

Regular Board Meeting

Thursday, September 24, 2015

5:30 P.M.

 

Proceedings of the Bogalusa School Board, taken at A Regular Meeting that was held on Thursday, September 4, 2015

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Regular Board Meeting at the Bogalusa City School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on Thursday, September 24, 2015 beginning at 5:30 P.M. The purpose of the meeting is to address information received from Department Supervisors, on Monday, September 22, 2015, during the Committee Meeting, regarding updates and approval requests.

 

1.)    Call to Order

 

2.)    Pledge of Allegiance

 

3.)    Prayer

 

4.)    Roll Call: X President Creel, X Vice President Kates, X Mrs. Duke, X Mr. Hymel, __ Mr. Kemp, __ Rev. Mims, __ Dr. Williams Approval of Minutes of Regular Board Meeting – August 27, 2015. It was motioned by Vice President Mr. Kates. A second was made by Mrs. Duke.  With three absent, the motion passed with unanimous approval.

 

5.)   Superintendent Willie E. “Toni” Breaux was absent due to illness and Mrs. Deloris Walker sat in in her absence.

 

6.)     Board Officer or Committee Reports – No reports were made.

 

7.)      Special Presentation regarding Partnership Agreement between ICON Performing Arts, Believe Summer Camp Experience, Washington Parish Community Arts Council and Bogalusa City Schools was given by Mr. Bruce Plummer

 

8.)     Mr. Calvin Hymel addressed the Meeting regarding discipline and left the meeting before public comments or before any business could be conducted.  Due to Mr. Hymel’s absence the meeting was adjourned at 6:00 p.m. due to there not being a quorum of School Board Members in attendance to continue.
