Bogalusa School Board

Bogalusa, Louisiana

Regular Board Meeting

December 18, 2013

5:30 P.M.

 

Proceedings Of The Bogalusa School Board, Taken AT A Regular Meeting That Was Held On Wednesday, December 18, 2013

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Regular Board Meeting at the Bogalusa School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on Wednesday, December 18, 2013 beginning at 5:30 P.M., for the purpose of addressing matters that were presented on Monday, December 16, 2013, at the Committee Meeting, regarding the end of 2013 that may have a district-wide impact on operations for 2014.  The agenda included a presentation, Election of Officers, and Oath of Office was administered to the elected officers, for 2014.

 

1.)        Call to Order

President Paul Kates called the meeting to order at 5:30 P.M.

 

2.)        Pledge of Allegiance

Superintendent Willie “Toni” Breaux led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

3.)        Prayer

Vice President Rev. Raymond Mims delivered the opening prayer.

 

4.)        Roll Call

There were present:  President, Mr. Paul Kates; Vice President, Rev. Raymond Mims; Members: Mr. Curtis Creel, Mrs. Eleanor Duke, Mr. Calvin Hymel, and Mr. Adam Kemp

 

There was absent:  Member:  Dr. Brad Williams

 

Statement from President Kates:  “Good evening everyone.  We want to welcome you all to our December, Regular Board Meeting.  At this time we want to have a presentation of the Bee Fit Logo and Coloring Contest winners; from Mrs. Joannie Miller.

 

5.)        Presentations: Bee Fit Logo And Coloring Contest Winners-Joannie Miller-CNP Director

 

Bee Fit Logo Winners

At this point Mrs. Miller came forward.  “Thank you for this opportunity to recognize our students in all the schools, in Bogalusa community, not just Bogalusa City Schools; but all of the schools.  We have this Bee Fit Heath Fair every year; this is Bee Fit IX, so this is our Ninth (9th) Annual Health Fair.  We do that for our community to let them know just how important health and physical education, and nutrition; how important those are to our everyday lives.  So, we congratulate you as the award winners; and we have a little presentation for you.  At this point she introduced Kathi Adams, RN for the district, who started the presentation with the Bee Fit Logo winners.  “This year we had a large number of entries.  There were so many that we decided this year, that we were going to recognize the four (4) outstanding individuals.”  The presentation was done in an inverted order going from Honorable Mention to First Place.  “While she’s giving her awards out, I would like to say one other thing that I failed to say.  This is in collaboration with LSU Bogalusa Medical Center, so we also have Merlin Duke here, who will be presenting awards in the formality of LSU Bogalusa Medical Center.  Merlin Duke, stated, “I am representing Lynn Wells, a Registered Nurse, who is Co-Chairman for this.”  Those receiving the awards were:

 

  • First Place-Tiyuana Myres-Bogalusa High School-10th Grade

  • Second Place-Kacie Morris-Bogalusa High School-11th Grade

  • Third Place-Maiya Hughes-Bogalusa High School-10th Grade

  • Honorable Mention-Ansley Knight-Bogalusa High School-8th Grade

 

At this point Mrs. Miller made the presentation to the Coloring Contest Winners.  Those receiving the awards were:

 

Bee Fit Coloring Contest Winners

 

  • Ja’nya Dillon-Byrd Avenue Primary School Grades K-1 Division & Overall Winner

  • Kimberly Newman-Central Elementary Grades 2-4  Division & Overall Winner

  • Hannah Traylor-Annunciation Catholic School Grades K-1 Division

  • Audrey Beebe-Annunciation Catholic School Grades 2-4 Division

  • Shelby Carney-Ben’s Ford Christian Academy Grades K-1 Division

  • Rhyon Craft-Ben’s Ford Christian Academy-Grades 2-4 Division

 

Award recipients were presented  with their original entry, award ribbon, personalized plaque, and a goodie bag filled with useful items from participating vendors that included such supplies as pens, cups, rulers, tote bag, etc.

 

At this point President Kates asked for a five (5) minutes recess to allow the participants of the Bee Fit Logo and Coloring Contest to exit for pictures, before resuming the agenda items.  Next on the agenda was Election of 2014 Officers.

 

6.)      Election Of 2014 Officers

 

  • President
    It was motioned by Rev. Mims, seconded by Mrs. Duke to nominate Mr. Adam Kemp for President of the Bogalusa City School Board, for 2014.  The motion was carried 6-0, with Board Member Dr. Brad Williams absent.

 

  • Vice President
    It was motioned by Mrs. Duke, seconded by Mr. Kemp to nominate Mr. Curtis Creel for Vice President of the Bogalusa City School Board, for 2014.  The motion was carried 6-0, with Board Member Dr. Brad Williams absent.

 

Statement from President Mr. Paul Kates-“At this time, I just want to thank this Board for working with me for the past three (3) years as being president.  I think we worked well together.  I think we’ve accomplished a lot of things together.  We’ve invested a lot of money in our schools.  We’ve remodeled them.  We’ve made them convenient for our children.  We tried to make everything convenient for all of our employees.  I just want to thank them.  I want to thank the staff, the superintendent and everybody that’s on this Board for coming together trying the move the Bogalusa City School System forward. We have a few stumbling blocks, but we’ll get over that. I just want to let the City of Bogalusa know, as a community, we all have to work together.  We can’t down the Bogalusa City School System.  We all have to work together and support the Bogalusa City School System.  I know they have a lot of people out there for Charter.  I know everybody have a choice to send their children wherever  they want to send their children, but in order for the Bogalusa City School System to  survive and move forward, we need all  of the kids in Bogalusa in our system.  I feel the people in Bogalusa should support the Bogalusa City School System.  I may not be saying a lot, but that’s the way I feel.  I think we’re on the right track.  We’re doing the best we can.  I know everyone read our last test scores; and they say we’re failing, yes, we’re failing but we’re striving to pass.  I don’t want Bogalusa City School System to die.  I just want to say once again, I thank you all for supporting me as president, and I want to congratulate Mr. Kemp for being President, and Mr. Creel for being Vice President.  I hope they have a successful year,” says President Kates.

Statement from Rev. Raymond Mims-“I’d just like to thank you Mr. Kates for your service to the Board.

 

7.)        Oath Of Office Of 2014 President Vice President

At this point Bogalusa City School Board’s attorney, Attorney Dale Branch, administered the Oath of Office to the 2014 newly elected Bogalusa City School Board officers, President Mr. Adam Kemp, and Vice President Mr. Curtis Creel.

 

8.)        Approval of “Revised” Minutes of Regular Board Meeting-October 30, 2013 (Line Item 10-D)

It was motioned by Rev. Mims, seconded by Mr. Hymel to approve the minutes.  The motion was carried 6-0, with Board Member Dr. Brad Williams absent.

 

9.)        Approval of Minutes of Regular Board Meeting-November 14, 2013

It was motioned by Mrs. Duke, seconded by Mr. Hymel to approve the minutes.  The motion was carried 6-0, with Board Member Dr. Brad Williams absent.

There were no reports, at this time.

No one came forward to address the Board or public.

14.)        Superintendent Comments-(Mrs.) Willie “Toni” Breaux

“First, I’d like to congratulate Mr. Adam Kemp as President of the Bogalusa City School Board, and I would like congratulate Mr. Curtis Creel as Vice President of Bogalusa City School Board.  I passed out to you all a sheet.  This was due to a meeting with the supervisors and myself, held on yesterday.  You can look at the sheet and we will discuss it at a later time, but I just wanted you to be aware of what we are doing, and that we are working on that.  Also, this is not going to be the end of the list, but I promised you on Monday night that I would, so, these are the things that we came up with as a group.  The last thing is we are working on survival skills to keep the Bogalusa City Schools alive. We had a meeting on that also this week.  The supervisors are here, and they can attest to this.  We are coming up with some problems with solutions, and I’ll get those to you as soon as they’re completed.  And finally, I would like to wish everybody a blessed holiday, and a prosperous New Year.  The last thing I have in the folder; you’re going to have two (2) things.  One is the Polar Express, All Aboard.  It is going to be held tomorrow at the Northside Community Resource Center from 4:30PM-6:30PM, and you’re all invited to attend.  The second thing in your folder is you know when we come back on January 6th, it’s not a day for students; it’s a Staff Development Day for the staff.  This is what we’re going to do; I am trying to motivate teachers and staff members, so what we’re doing is we’re having a motivational speaker to come in.  He is going to speak to every staff member that’s employed by the Bogalusa City School System.  That’s all I have.  Thank you,” concluded Superintendent Breaux.

   

15.)        Board Members Comments

Rev. Raymond Mims-“Congratulations to you both; and I just want to wish everybody a Merry Christmas, and a Happy and Prosperous New Year.”

 

Mrs. Eleanor Duke-“I’d like to congratulate you too, and I hope you have a wonderful year.”

 

Mr. Adam Kemp-“I’d just like to thank you for the confidence in taking on this position.  I look forward to the next year, and hope for the positive change we can make.  I surely feel that one person is not going to make a difference.  Everybody on this Board has worked well together, and I don’t feel that we will change in any way.  Again, I look forward to working for the school system.  I will always be available.  I will do my best to contact everyone to let them know of things going on.  I look forward to working with Mrs. Breaux and her staff as well, in trying to move Bogalusa forward.  I am determined to do the best that I can and working with Mrs. Creel as well.  Again, I look forward to this, and I appreciate your confidence.”

 

Mr. Curtis Creel-“I appreciate your vote of confidence as position of Vice President.  I feel sure that nothing is going to change, because I feel like we’re all on a team to work together and on one accord when we can, as a Board for the system.  We have created a clean, safe, conducive environment for our children, and I’m looking forward to the future, for the educational process to come around and be a vocal point that people will see that the City of Bogalusa School System is not dying; we still have a heartbeat, and we’re going to move forward.  This is going to be a good year.  I am confident; this is going to be a good year.  Thank y’all,” concluded Mr. Creel.

 

Mr. Paul Kates-“At this time, I just want to say that I wish everyone a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year; and tell the City of Bogalusa, that Bogalusa City Schools will survive,”
