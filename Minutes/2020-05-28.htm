8.)    Superintendent Tanner Comments –

Superintendent Tanner began by letting the board know that she and all of the essential and volunteer staff members have worked very hard to make sure BCS was following all protocols set forth by the Governor and LA Department of Education. Everyone has been on phone conferences, Zoom meetings and such multiple times per day, every day.  She again emphasized that her focus in the beginning of the pandemic was making sure all of the children were fed and our CNP staff and feeding volunteers were protected. Superintendent Tanner let the board know that we have an application on our website where parents can begin registering their children for virtual learning.  Within 24 hours we have had 118 register and some of those were from Charter, Private Schools and even local children who were out of our system.  She continued by expressing how she would never do anything detrimental to our system and everyone thing she does she believes is in the best interest of students and personnel.  Superintendent continued by saying she is one person and if there are any questions any board member may have, they should call her and asked her before placing judgement. She also stated that each principal should be reaching out to their students/parents and letting them know the status of what will be going on as we move forward, whether it be athletics, extracurricular activities or fund raising events.  Superintendent Tanner ended by informing the board that the JROTC building will be used for Covid-19 testing beginning next week for all.  There will be 2 sites in the west parish and 2 in the east parish.

 

9.)    Board Comments – Mrs. Breaux began by letting everyone know she was not being critical of anything or anyone but she is truly scared of Covid-19.  She also wanted to confirm that there will continue to be temperature checks and handwashing and disinfecting done by students and staff until this pandemic is under control.  She believes we all need to take this seriously. Mrs. Duke, Mr. Killingsworth, Mr. Kemp and Dr. Williams followed Mrs. Breaux by congratulating Superintendent Tanner and her essential employees for the great work they have continued to do for BSC during these unprecedented times.

 

10.) Meeting adjourned at 7:45 p.m.
