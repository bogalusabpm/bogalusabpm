Bogalusa School Board Office-Media Center

Bogalusa, Louisiana

Regular Board Meeting

Thursday, July 30, 2020

5:30 p.m.

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Regular Board Meeting at the Bogalusa City School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on July 30, 2020. The purpose of the meeting is to address information received from Department Supervisors, on July 27, 2020, during the Committee Meeting, regarding updates and approval requests.

9.)       New Business

a.     Approval of June, 2020 Revised Budgets and Financial Statements; General Fund, Food Services, Special Revenue and School Activities. It was motioned by Dr. Williams. A second was made by Mr. Kemp. The motion passed with unanimous approval.

b.    Approval of Policy Update: 2020, Equal Opportunity Employment, Title VII Employee Sexual Harassment, Title IX Sexual Harassment, Non-Title IX Complaints and Grievances, Investigations and Sexually Related Student Misconduct. It was motioned by Mr. Kemp. A second was made by Mr. Creel. The motion passed with unanimous approval.

c.     Approval of 2020-2021 School Calendar revision later start to school year. Superintendent Tanner explained there are two calendars: Calendar #1 has the children starting school after Labor Day, September 8, with no Fair Holiday or Mardi Gras Holidays and school would end on June 11th.  Calendar #2 starts August 31st which gives us another week and we would get out of school June 7th with no Fair Holiday, Mardi Gras Holiday and 2 days taken from Christmas Holidays ending school on June 7h. 60.7% of the staff voted for Calendar 2. BHS will take in earlier and they will get out at 3:05. Byrd Avenue and Central start arriving at 7:55 with BHS start arriving at 7:45. BHS will be taking in before the elementary schools.  The school days are not any longer. BHS will let out at 3:05 which allows to pick up at High School first and then proceed to elementary schools.  We will meet required minutes with both calendars.

 

Roll Call Vote as follows:

 

Calendar 1: Y – Kates, N – Killingsworth, N – Breaux, Y – Creel, N – Duke, Y – Kemp, N – Williams N-4 Y-3

 

Calendar 2: N – Kates, Y – Killingsworth, N – Breaux, N – Creel, Y – Duke, N – Kemp, Y – Williams N-4 Y-3

 

Since there was a tie President Kates called to for Calendar 1. Beginning September 8th ending June 11th.
