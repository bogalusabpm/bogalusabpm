Proceedings of the Bogalusa School Board taken at A Regular Meeting that was held on Wednesday, June 15, 2016.

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Regular Board Meeting at the Bogalusa City School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on Wednesday, June 15, 2016 beginning at 5:30 P.M. The purpose of the meeting is to address information received from Department Supervisors, on Monday, June 13, 2016, during the Committee Meeting, regarding updates and approval requests.
