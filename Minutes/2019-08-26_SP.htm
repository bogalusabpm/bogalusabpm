MONDAY, AUGUST, 26, 2019

 

Proceedings of the Bogalusa School Board taken at the Special Meeting that was held on Monday, August 26, 2019.

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Special Board Meeting at the Bogalusa City School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on August 26, 2019 following the Regular Committee Meeting. The purpose of the meeting was to address information received from Deloris Walker, Financial Director, regarding Revenue Anticipation Loan.

 

1.)         Call to Order – Vice President Kates

 

2.)         Pledge of Allegiance – Superintendent Lisa Tanner

 

3.)         Prayer – Dr. H. Brad Williams

 

4.)         Roll Call:  A President Creel, X Vice President Kates, _X_ Mrs. Breaux, X Mrs. Duke, X Mr. Kemp, X Mr. Killingsworth, X Dr. Williams

 

5.)         Approval of Revenue Anticipation Loan. It was motioned by Mr. Kemp. A second was made by Dr. Williams. The motion passed with unanimous approval.

 

A Roll Call Vote was taken:
