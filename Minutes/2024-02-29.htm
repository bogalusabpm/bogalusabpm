D.   Superintendent Report

§  A salary consulting agency will come in to look at our salaries and compare them to the number of employees we have; neighboring districts and districts of the same size, to make sure we have equitable salaries across all positions.

§  A Charter Accountability Committee has been formed to look at the finances, academics, and operations.

§   The State Wide Monitoring found no compliance issues in several areas: C-Cap B3, Career and Technical Education, Comprehensive Literacy State Development, Direct Student Services, Our Cares Act Funding, Individual Disability Act, and LA 4 Program.

§   An Employee Advisory Council is being formed.

§   161 students are participating in the Friday tutoring: Byrd Avenue - 36, Central Elementary - 54, and Bogalusa High School - 71.

§   The new board system (Simbli) is now on Bogalusa City Schools website

§   The JROTC Ball is on Saturday at 6:00 p.m. Please come out to support our students.  The CapitalOne Financial Literacy Sessions was today with high school seniors.

§   ACT Boot Camp at high school.

§   Extracurricular school activity expenses.
