Proceedings of the Bogalusa School Board taken at A Regular Meeting that was held on Thursday, APRIL 28, 2016.

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Regular Board Meeting at the Bogalusa City School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on Thursday, April 28, 2016 beginning at 5:30 P.M. The purpose of the meeting is to address information received from Department Supervisors, on Monday, April 25, 2016, during the Committee Meeting, regarding updates and approval requests.

 

1.)  Call to Order

2.)  Pledge of Allegiance

3.)  Prayer

4.)   Roll Call: X President Creel, X Vice President Kates, A Mrs. Duke, X Mr. Hymel, X Mr. Kemp,   A  Rev. Mims,   X  Dr. Williams

 

5.)    Approval of Minutes of Regular Board Meeting March 25, 2016. It was motioned by Mr. Kemp. A second was made by Dr. Williams. The motion passed with unanimous approval.

 

6.)  Presentation of Student of the Year Awards

a.      Central Elementary – Amani Turner

b.      BHS 6th – 8th – Maria Martinez

c.      BHS 9th – 12th – Miya Tate

 

7.)  Bogalusa School Based Health Center Presentation

 

8.)  Board Officers and Committee Reports

 

9.)  Recess To Public Session

 

10.)         New Business

a. Approval of Financial Statements and Revised Budgets, March, 2016: General Fund, Food Service, Special

b. Revenue and School Activities (Deloris Walker) It was motioned by Mr. Kates. A second was made by Dr. Williams.  The motion passed with unanimous approval.

c.  Approval of Sales Tax Checks percentage 170.11% (Deloris Walker) It was motioned by Mr. Hymel. A second was made by Dr. Williams.  The motion passed with unanimous approval.

d.  Approval of District Consolidation Plan for 2016-2017 (Willie E. “Toni” Breaux) The motion was made by Mr. Kemp. Motion failed for lack of a second motion.

e. Superintendent Evaluation (President, Curtis Creel).  Superintendent agreed to accept evaluation as given.

 

9.)  Superintendent Comments – (Willie E. Toni Breaux)
