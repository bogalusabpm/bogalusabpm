Proceedings of the Bogalusa School Board taken at A Regular Meeting that was held on Thursday, January 27, 2016.

The purpose of the meeting is to address information received from Department Supervisors, on Monday, January 25, 2016, during the Committee Meeting, regarding updates and approval requests.
