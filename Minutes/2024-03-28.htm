F.       Superintendent Report

Dr. Hurst asked Charlotte Tillman, Chief Academic Officer, to speak concerning the Bogalusa City Schools Pre K/LA4 Program Registration (see attached handout):

  • A child must be 4 years old on or before September 30th, live within the attendance boundary of the school, and meet income requirements.

  • Eligibility is based on income

  • The program is child-centered

  • High quality and developmentally appropriate Tier I Curriculum is implemented, so that each child enters kindergarten ready for success

  • Full-day attendance program

  • Low student to teacher ratios/smaller class sizes

  • Supports and provides services for children with identified disabilities

  • Transition meeting to kindergarten

  • Follows objectives from Teaching Strategies (TS) Gold

  • Registration will take place Monday, April 8th - Friday, April 12th at the Bogalusa City Schools Central Office from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.; and May

  • 100 seats are available

  • There are 5 General Education classrooms and 1 Special Education classroom located at Byrd Avenue Primary School
