SPECIAL BOARD MEETING

MONDAY, JUNE 11, 2018

4:00 P.M.

 

Proceedings of the Bogalusa School Board taken at the Special Meeting that was held on MONDAY, JUNE 11, 2018.

 

The Bogalusa School Board met in a Special Board Meeting at the Bogalusa City School Board Office Media Center, located at 1705 Sullivan Drive, Bogalusa, Louisiana, on JUNE 11, 2018.

 

1.)        Call to Order – President Creel

 

2.)        Pledge of Allegiance – Superintendent Lisa Tanner

 

3.)        Prayer – Vice President Paul Kates

 

4.)        Roll Call:  X President Creel, X Vice President Kates, X Mrs. Duke, X Mr. Hymel,  X  Mr. Kemp,  

                         A Rev. Mims, A Dr. Williams

 

5.)        Board Officers and Committee Reports – NONE

 

6.)        Recess to Public Session – NONE

 

7.)        Approval of Resolution to issue a Revenue Anticipation Note was presented by Deloris Walker.  A motion was made by Vice President Kates and a second by Mr. Kemp to Approve the Resolution. A vote followed:

 

Yes – President Creel, Yes – Vice President Kates, Yes – Mrs. Duke, Yes – Mr. Hymel, Yes – Mr. Kemp

 

With 5 present and 2 absent the motion passed with unanimous approval

 

8.)           Approval to open negotiations to request to modify or terminate the agreement with the Union AFLCIO (Local #776, Council 17) was presented by Deloris Walker on behalf of Karla McGehee.  A motion was made by Vice President Kates and a second by Mrs. Duke to approve.  The motion passed with unanimous approval

 

9.)           Superintendent Tanner Comments – Ask for prayers for Missy Moses, Lorene Randazzo, Shelley Gill’s Husband and Cynthia August.  She also announced the return of Tonyah Varnado-Seal.

 

10.)          Committee Meeting and Board Meetings for June will both be held on Monday, June 25, 2018 at 5:30 p.m.

 

11.)          Board Members Comments – None.

 

12.)          Adjourn
