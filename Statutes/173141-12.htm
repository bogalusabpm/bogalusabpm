LOUISIANA REVISED STATUTES

TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 24-A. PROPRIETARY SCHOOLS

 

17:3141.12     Recovery on contracts

 

No recovery shall be had on any contract for or in connection with a course of instruction by any owner selling or administering such course if the solicitor for such owner was not the holder of a valid permit as required by the provisions of this chapter at the time the contract was negotiated or the course sold.
