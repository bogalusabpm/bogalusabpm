TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 5. STATE COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES

PART III. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

 

17:1813    Computer literacy center; Northwestern State University at Natchitoches; authority to operate

  A. Northwestern State University at Natchitoches is hereby authorized to establish and operate a computer literacy center. The establishment and operation of the center shall be subject to the constitutional authority of the Board of Regents and the Board of Trustees for State Colleges and Universities.

  B. Northwestern State University at Natchitoches is further authorized to utilize all of its colleges or departments in operating the center and providing needed services.

Added by Acts 1984, No. 152, Sec. 1.
