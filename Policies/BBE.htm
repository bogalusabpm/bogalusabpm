BOARD ATTORNEY

 

 

Legal affairs of the school district shall be handled by the Bogalusa City School Board and Superintendent with the aid and assistance of a qualified attorney appointed by the School Board.  The role of the School Board attorney shall be to propose, check, review and approve all legal documents pertaining to the School Board and/or school district.  The attorney shall also be available for consultation with the School Board and/or Superintendent on all matters of a legal nature.

 

 

Ref:    La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §§16:2, 42:261.1
