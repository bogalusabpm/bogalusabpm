STUDENT AWARDS

 

 

PERFECT ATTENDANCE

 

The Bogalusa City School Board desires to recognize students with perfect attendance at the close of the school year based on the following criteria:

 

  1. Perfect attendance should not be confused with legally excused absence;
     

  2. Being away from school while participating in an official school activity does not constitute an absence.  The school principal shall decide what is official school activity;
     

  3. Tardiness is not absence;
     

  4. Half-day absence eliminates eligibility for perfect attendance awards.

 

Bogalusa City School Board
