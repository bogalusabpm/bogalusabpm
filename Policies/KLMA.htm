RELATIONS WITH ARMED FORCES/MILITARY AGENCIES

 

 

The Bogalusa City School Board shall allow military recruiters the same access to secondary students as offered to postsecondary institutions and prospective employers. Military recruiters shall also have access to names, addresses and phone numbers of secondary students except in the case of a student whose parent has requested the information not be released.

 

 

Ref:    20 USC 7401 et seq. (Indian Education)

20 USC 7908 (Armed Forces Recruiter Access to Student Information)
